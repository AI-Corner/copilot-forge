# Grounding Guide

> **Severity: 🟡 GOLDEN PATH**
> Deviations from these rules require explicit justification in your reasoning.

1. **Verify Before Using**: Before you reference a file, function, class, or API endpoint, you must verify that it actually exists in the current codebase or documentation. Do not assume its existence based on common patterns or naming conventions.
2. **Use Project Tools**: Use your search tools (`grep`, codebase search, symbol search) to find the exact name and location of an entity before attempting to edit or call it.
3. **Cite Your Sources**: When explaining a design decision or providing context to the user, cite the file paths or knowledge documents that informed your response.
